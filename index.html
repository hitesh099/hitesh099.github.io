About Me

I'm a Performance & Observability Engineer with 5+ years of experience building enterprise monitoring platforms and executing performance testing strategies for large-scale cloud, microservices, and containerized systems.

At RadixBit Technologies, I owned the full observability lifecycle — replacing legacy Wily and Teamquest tools with Dynatrace, deploying OneAgent across 1,000+ VMs and 10+ Kubernetes/OpenShift clusters, and leading Dynatrace Managed → SaaS migrations. I also ran end-to-end performance testing for major cloud migration projects, integrating testing directly into CI/CD pipelines.

My dual expertise in observability (Dynatrace, Grafana, Prometheus, RUM, Synthetics) and performance engineering (JMeter, LoadRunner, JVM tuning, capacity planning) gives me a full-stack view that most engineers in either discipline alone don't have.

Graduated with an MS in Computer Science from UAB in May 2025, with research focused on scalability analysis and observability frameworks for cloud-native microservices.
